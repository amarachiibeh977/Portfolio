Customer Churn Analysis using POWER BI

In this project, I analyzed a customer churn dataset for a travel company, focusing on identifying key churn drivers and formulating strategies to reduce attrition. Using Power BI, I developed an interactive dashboard to visualize critical KPIs including churn rate, customer segmentation by age and income, and engagement with key services like hotel bookings. The analysis revealed actionable insights such as high churn rates among customers aged 27-38 and income-driven patterns, allowing me to recommend targeted retention strategies. Sales Analysis using EXCEL

I explored video game sales data from 1980 to 2017 to uncover trends and key insights into the gaming industry. Using Excel and pivot tables, I cleaned and organized the data, then created an interactive dashboard to highlight findings. The analysis revealed that Action games dominate the market, with the highest percentage of games and sales by genre. The year 2008 recorded the highest annual sales, and top publishers like Nintendo and Electronic Arts emerged as industry leaders.
